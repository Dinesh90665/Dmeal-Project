Help & Questions
How do I place an order?
To place an order, browse the menu, add items to your cart, and then proceed to checkout. You can pay online or choose cash on delivery.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
You can cancel or modify your order within 5 minutes of placing it. Go to your account orders section and select “Modify” or “Cancel”.
What should I do if my food is delayed?
Sometimes delays happen due to traffic or weather. You can track your order in real-time from your dashboard or contact support.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes, we use secure encryption and trusted gateways (eSewa, Fonepay) to ensure your data is safe.
How can I contact customer support?
You can reach us via the chat button in the app or email us at support@dmeal.com. We're available 24/7.
