Security Policy

Dmeal Restaurant is committed to maintaining the security of your personal information and providing a safe dining and online experience.

1. Data Protection: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal and payment information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

2. Secure Payment Processing: All online and in-person payments are processed securely via trusted payment gateways using encryption.

3. Access Control: Access to customer data is restricted to authorized employees only and is regularly reviewed.

4. Physical Security: Our premises are monitored to prevent unauthorized access and ensure the safety of your personal belongings.

5. Website Security: Our website uses HTTPS to encrypt data transmitted between your device and our servers.

6. Incident Response: We have procedures in place to respond promptly to any data breaches or security incidents.

7. Employee Training: Staff are trained on best security practices and the importance of protecting customer data.
